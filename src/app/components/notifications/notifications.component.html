Notifications

Notifications are the possible content of observables. They ocurre over time and are a representation of any particular thing. Notifications can have:

  • Color
  • Shape
  • Content (proper contrast to fill color)
  • Width (in full blocks)

Color and content combination should always have a proper contrast. Find a onlinetool here: Contrast checker.

The value color can be only black or white. With is here to provide enough space for more content than just 1 chars. Every shape can have a dynamic width.
