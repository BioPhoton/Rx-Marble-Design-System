Time Span

Time Span is here to specify a certain duration of time. The smallest possible size is 1 turn in the event loop. The description of the time span is placed inside the box and consists out of an amount directly followed by a unit. No space inbetween. The amount is a number. The unit, as singular, can be any known unit of time as well as the unit frame, which is equal to one block. For time units you can use shortcuts as ms for milliseconds or s for seconds as well as full name i.e. month or year. Of course you can also use any other imaginary unit of time like a moment ;-)
