Color

All colors are are barrier free and used for specific things in the diagram. There are 3 fixed colors and 4 to n colors that are free to choose. For Text, lines, and arrows the “line” color is used. The “background” color is not only used for the background but also for text to get an acceptable color ratio. You can check your pallet here. The “inactive” color is used for anything that should be recognized as inactive.

Color Palettes

Colors serve the purpose to distinguish between notifications or observables. Try to use as less colors as possible to keep the visual noise of a marble diagram low. If you create your own color patterns keep in mind that you can use text inside shapes.

Also always check your palette including text with a color contrast tool like accessible-color-matrix.

For “notification” colors you can use the defined colors 1 - 4 or any other color of your choice. All notification colors have the same meaning. There is no “danger” or “success” color.
